Your journey starts here

Once you have started on your journey with us, you'll find more opportunities than we can list here.
As an open-source project, the community's contributions are what moves things forward.

Contribute to the project
The X-Cash Foundation is running several bounty programs and sponsoring for small to big projects relating to the X-Cash ecosystem. You can participate in the development, be an integral part of the project and earn a reward for doing so..

Join the Community Weekly Voice Event...
And have your say on the direction of the project, or show what you've been working on, every Wednesday at 7pm UTC+2 on Discord.

Become a delegate
The X-Cash blockchain is governed by elected delegates securing the network. Providing people vote for you, or that you have enough XCASH to get you elected, you can also become an elected delegate and start forging blocks.
