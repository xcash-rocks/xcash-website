The X-Cash Foundation

The open-source X-Cash Project is developed by the X-Cash Foundation:
a non-profit organization created for the sole purpose of managing X-Cash.

  • Open-source ‍‍
    X-Cash is an open-source project, built upon the work of skilled and engaged contributors. Every project around the X-Cash Ecosystem is open for contributions by the public GitHub.
  • Community-driven
    Following a clear line of technical development, the vision is built with the community, by the community. Every project proposed by the community is assessed and evaluated by the foundation to provide funding.
  • Self-Funded
    The project has been funded by the founding team, and there was no ICO. The current development of the cryptocurrency is now payed with the community fund, put in place to reward projects built for the X-Cash ecosystem.
