Eco-X

Like Night and Day
Unlike Proof-of-Work blockchains, or POS blockchains which require wallets to remain open, X-Cash's DPOPS Blockchain does not, therefore significantly reducing network-wide energy consumption making it ideal for building applications.

Eco-Proof
Our use of reserve proofs remove the need for devices to remain open to stake, therefore giving the voted-for delegate the task of distributing rewards: reward status can be verified by plugging into our delegate explorer API.

Our Claim
The X-Cash blockchain is estimated to use less energy than 4 Toasters. However, while we're confident that this estimate is appropriate, we encourage you to dispute this.
