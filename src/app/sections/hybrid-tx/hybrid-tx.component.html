Public/Private Transactions

The user is the only authority on their funds, and we provide the tools.

Flex-Private send
One single wallet on our blockchain is used to perform public and private transactions. This enables anonymity to be used when privacy is needed while allowing for transparent transactions when required.

Flex-Private receive
X-Cash offers the choice to receive only private or only public transactions to an address, and of course just like private send, unconditional transactions are default.

Private Stake
Always putting the user experience first, X-Cash is the only blockchain to offer this feature.

Secured & Unfalsifiable
private and public transactions are cryptographically signed in large fixed ring sized transactions.
