X-Bank

The X-Bank is a secured off-chain online service to store
and access swap services for the X-Cash blockchain.

  • X-Bank - Free Bridge, Forever
    We have built the bridge from the X-Cash Mainnet to the Polygon Network in X-Bank: the free & secure XCASH storage solution. You can easily swap your Mainnet XCASH to Polygon XCASH, instantaneously and for free.
  • Highly Secured
    With a combinaison of multi-signatures wallets, automatic cold wallet storage and a private dedicated network, the security of your funds is our priority.
  • Store and Use as needed
    You can safely store your XCASH in X-Bank's secured storage solution. Transfers within the X-Bank are free, and we only charging standard blockchain fees for withdrawal.
  • Swap to WXCASH
    Create an account on the X-Bank to safely store your XCASH, and proceed with the integrated free swap feature to change your XCASH to WXCASH.
