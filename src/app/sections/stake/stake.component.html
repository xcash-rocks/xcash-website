Staking
The days of checking if your computer is online staking are gone. By voting with your proof of funds, you're voting for a node to act in the interest of the network, who'll reward you for your vote.

So turn off your device and let your reserve proof do all the work.

Stake Delegation with Reserve Proofs
Never send your coins to earn a stake. Do however send a cryptographic reserve proof of your funds to your delegate, and retain complete control of your coins while your proof of funds works to increase your balance.

Your reserve proof (proof of funds) is auto-generated.

Randomized Block Producer Selection
With the presence of Verifiable random functions (VRF), rest assured that you have an equal chance of receiving a stake with any other staking wallet.

VRFs force true random and unanticipatable delegate block producer selection, thus increasing our network's security by removing the chance of an attack on the next elected delegate node.
