Staking
The days of constantly checking that your computer is up staking are long gone. By voting with your proof of XCASH reserves, you're voting for a delegate node to act in the interest of the network, and in turn you receive stake rewards.

With your reserve proof doing all the work, turn off your device and enjoy your worry-free day.

Randomized Block Producer Selection
With the presence of Verifiable random functions (VRF), rest assured that you have equal chance of receiving a stake with any other staking wallet.

VRFs force true random and unanticipatable delegate block producer selection, which also bolsters our network's security by removing the chance of attack on the next elected delegate node.

Stake Delegation with Reserve Proofs
Instead of sending your stake to another wallet to vote toward a delegate, you are providing a cryptographic reserve proof of your funds to your delegate, meaning that you keep complete control of your stake at all times.
