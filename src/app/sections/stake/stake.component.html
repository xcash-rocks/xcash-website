Randomized Block Producer Selection
With the presence of Verifiable random functions (VRF), you can rest assured that you have equal chance of receiving a stake with any other staking wallet. VRF force true random and unanticipatable delegate block producer selection, which also bolsters our network's security by removing the chance of attack on the next elected delegate node.

Stake Delegation with Reserve Proofs
Instead of sending your stake to another wallet to vote toward a delegate, you are providing a cryptographic reserve proof of your funds to your delegate, meaning that you keep complete control of your stake at all times.
