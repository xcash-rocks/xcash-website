Securely store your XCASH

To start using X-Cash, you will need your own secured wallet. Because we want to make it as easy as possible for everyone, we have designed multiple solutions for you to store your XCASH securely.

Desktop Wallet Mobile Wallet X-Bank Paper Wallet

Desktop Wallet

The desktop wallet is the easiest and most secure way to access your XCASH and manage your funds.

  • Manage all your XCASH wallets in one place.
  • Vote for the delegate of your choice.
  • Check your transfer history and register addresses for easy access.

Mobile Wallet

Securely access your XCASH wherever you are! The mobile wallets were designed to enable you to send XCASH from the comfort of your phone.

  • Connect to a secure node and access your wallets.
  • Restore and import your existing wallets using your seeds.
  • Vote for the delegate of your choice, directly from your phone.

X-Bank

The X-Bank is a community project actively maintained by the X-Cash Foundation. It serves as an online custody solution to securely store and manage your XCASH, and will be the bridge to the XCASH ERC-20 token : wXCASH.

  • Create an account in a few seconds.
  • Transfer XCASH easily and instantaneously. FREE when sending to another X-Bank account.
  • Participate in the consensus and vote for delegates.
