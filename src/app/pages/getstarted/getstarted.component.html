Use Them

The community is building new products and new ways to use XCASH.

X-Payment
A payment solution that can be instantaneously integrated on any Discord server. Send some XCASH over there, participate in the discussions and easily send funds to anyone on the same server.

Vote and earn XCASH
The X-Cash Network is governed by delegates elected by the community. You can use your XCASH to vote for a delegate, and be retributed for your stake.

Get Involved

Once you have started your journey with X-Cash, there is still much you can do to get more involved.
As an open-source project, X-Cash relies on contributions to move forward.

Contribute to the project
The X-Cash Foundation is running several bounty programs and sponsoring for small to big projects relating to the X-Cash ecosystem. You can participate in the development, be an integral part of the project and earn a consequent reward to do so.

Become a delegate
The X-Cash blockchain is governed by elected delegates securing the network. Providing people vote for you, or that you have enough XCASH to get you elected, you can also become an elected delegate and start forging blocks.
