People at X-Cash

X-Cash is a blockchain platform for changemakers, innovators, and visionaries, with the tools and technologies required to create possibility for the many, as well as the few, and bring about positive global change.
Developers, businesses, artists, and everyday users around the world are working together to build the digital flexprivacy commonwealth.
Come join the global X-Cash community.
‍

What we believe in:

  • We are an open-source and self-funded project that aims at building technology first.
  • We respect opinions, ideologies and engage in constructive discussions.
  • As contributors, we are the first ambassadors of the project and its values. Having outstanding written skills is essential to have a clear communication with people from within the community and out.

Join us and be part of this adventure with us.
