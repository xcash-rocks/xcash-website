Partnerships

The community is building new products and new ways to use XCASH.

X-Payment
A payment solution that can be instantaneously integrated on any Discord server. Send some XCASH over there, participate in the discussions and easily send funds to anyone on the same server.

Vote and earn XCASH
The X-Cash Network is governed by delegates elected by the community. You can use your XCASH to vote for a delegate, and be retributed for your stake.
