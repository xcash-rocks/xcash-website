Open Contributor Positions

You are looking to contribute to the project but you are not a developer? You are at the right place.
The X-Cash Foundation also needs engaged people to help manage, promote, and improve X-Cash.

Apply to a contributor position and become part of the X-Cash Foundation.

Open Positions

Project Manager

Missions

We are looking to onboard a contributor with project management skills to develop the X-Cash contributor program and help us supervise it.

As the Project Manager, you will be responsible for:

-         Finalizing the contribution guidelines with the other contributors

-         Define the contribution workflow in GitHub Project

-         Follow up and update the existing pipeline of projects

- Apply and enforce the contribution guidelines for healthy repositories

-         Attend the contributor calls and provide updates to the rest of contributors

-         Coordinate with the communication team to provide articles and content to update the community

 

Contributor Profile

-         Previous relevant experiences in project management

-         Knowledge of the X-Cash Core technology and principles

-         Understanding of GitHub workflow and GitHub Projects

-         Great communication and written skills (English)

-         Proactive and Curious

-         Being already active in the X-Cash community is a strong plus

 

Retribution & Terms

This task is retribution 350 USD XCASH equivalent per month, capped at 20USD/MXCASH. The retribution is paid between the 25-31st of each month. After a 6 month period,  possibility of integrating the team fund reward.

The position is indefinitely renewable in two-months cycles. XCASH valuation is taken at the time of the payment, from Coingecko live data. Payment will be made using X-Cash‚Äôs public transaction for auditability. 

 

Application Process

You are interested? Please provide your application before April, 30th 2021.

Your application should contain:

  • A short summary about you and how you are the most able to succeed in this position.
  • Contact information (Discord ID, mail, name)


The final decision results will be shared by the X-Cash Foundation committee during the next weekly contributor call.
