Governance & Consensus

The X-Cash Public Network is governed by 50 delegates through a custom Delegated Proof of Stake consensus designed on top of the Monero technology, the Delegated Proof of Private Stake (DPOPS).

  • Secured by the top 50 elected delegates
    Anyone can run an X-Cash node and become a delegate. But to be able to forge new blocks, secure the network, and earn the block reward, you will need to be voted into the top 50 by other members of the community.‚Äå Becoming a delegate is ambitious; reaching and keeping a forging spot is rewarding but can be taken out at any time.
  • Staking & Voting
    The delegates are elected by the community. Each people can attribute a stake of a minimum of 2M¬†XCASH to a delegate to represent a weighted vote. Delegates then distribute a fraction of the block reward to their voters to remunerate them.
    Additionally, the voters' stake never leave their wallet thanks to the use of cryptographic reserve proofs; you stay in control of your XCASH at all time.
  • Improved security
    Using a DBFT¬†communication system to guarantee a 67% consensus, and a verifiable randomized selection of a block producer to avoid delegates targeted attacks, everything has been implemented to improve the security of the network.

Technologies

Our mission is to build a technological framework made for the web3 privacy.

  • Hybrid Transactions
    We are offering the transparency of bitcoin transactions with Monero's obfuscation to allow most privacy needs to be addressed.
  • Delegated-Proof-of-Private-Stake
    We are proposing an innovative variant of the dPoS consensus protocol combining several innovation:
    - Privacy-switch for stake used in the voting process
    - Byzantine Fault Tolerant algorithm
    - Verifiable Random Functions for the block producer selection process
  • Sidechains & Smart Contracts
    Following the layer-1 of our technology, we are developing the second layer that will allow both scalability and increase in use cases. The delegates can offer also host sidechains and execute smart-contracts which generates additional yields.
