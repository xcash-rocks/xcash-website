Contribute to the X-Cash Project

The X-Cash Foundation is managing the development of the X-Cash Project, providing funding for community-driven projects and bounties for necessary developments.
‚Äç
Join us and be part of this adventure with us.

What we believe in:

  • We are an open-source and self-funded project who aims at building technology first.
  • We respect opinions, ideologies and engage in constructive discussions.
  • As contributors, we are the first ambassadors of the project and its values. Having outstanding written skills is essential to have a clear communication with people from within the community and out.

Builder's Project

The Builder's Project is a special bounty program run by the X-Cash Foundation, where contributors and developers can undertake special tasks and development projects to improve the X-Cash Project and earn subsequent rewards.

Builder's Project are listed here, and on our official GitHub repository.
